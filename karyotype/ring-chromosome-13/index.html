Ring chromosome 13

46,XY,r(13)(p11q34)

Also known as: r(13), ring 13

46,XY,r(13)(p11q34) is a ring chromosome 13, formed when both arms of chromosome 13 break and the broken ends fuse into a circle. Ring chromosomes are unstable through cell division.

What the notation means

46
total chromosome count (the normal human number)
XY
sex chromosomes: one X, one Y (usual male karyotype)
r(13)(p11q34)
a RING chromosome r(13): the chromosome's arms break and the broken ends fuse into a circle (usually loses the distal tips)
