Normal male karyotype

46,XY

Also known as: 46 XY

46,XY is the standard male karyotype: 46 chromosomes made up of 22 pairs of autosomes, one X, and one Y. It is the baseline that every abnormal karyotype is read against.

What the notation means

46
total chromosome count (the normal human number)
XY
sex chromosomes: one X, one Y (usual male karyotype)
