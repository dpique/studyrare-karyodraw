# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

In the interest of a welcoming and harassment-free community, everyone who
participates in KaryoDraw (through issues, pull requests, or any other channel)
agrees to make participation a respectful experience for all, regardless of age,
body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment: using welcoming
and inclusive language, respecting differing viewpoints, accepting constructive
feedback gracefully, and focusing on what is best for the community. Unacceptable
behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or
political attacks, and publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or
contacting the maintainer through the repository at
<https://github.com/dpique/studyrare-karyodraw>. Maintainers may remove, edit, or
reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
